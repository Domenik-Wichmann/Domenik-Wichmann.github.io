Example Sale Data

This is one year's worth of sample sales data from my business, Juno Farms.
Using this data, we will be able to extrapolate best sellers, keep track of inventory,
and generate simple visualizations

Go to Sheet

How it works

Product Info

Each product that the business sells has 4 main attrubites: Pruduct Name, Variant, Size, and Price. The size and price are defined in a table. The user can then enter all the products into a seperate table, which will pull the price and size automatically. After the main attributes are all filled in, a human-readable SKU will be automatically generated. A label and a QR code will be generated as well. The these are both printed on the price tags of the products, and used for tracking inventory.

Data Collection

In the 'Sales' tab of the sheet, a date range can be selected. The sheet will then calculate how many of each item was sold during that period, how much each item grossed, the gross revenue, and the net.

The sales are formatted in a wide table, with the dates and location in the header of each column, and the sales underneath


Data Analysis

In the 'Sales' tab, a date range can be selected.

The sheet will automatically calulate the total number of each item sold, the total ounces, how much each item grossed, the gross revenue, and the net.

The sheet will allow you to see exact sales during any time period, including the amount of any given ingredient that was sold as a mix with other ingredients. The cells are color coded to make spotting trends easy.

The sheet will also automatically generate some simple visualisations.

The data will then be compiled in the 'Restock' tab to provide a printer-friendly list of items that need to be restocked.
