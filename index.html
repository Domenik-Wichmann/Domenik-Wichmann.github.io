I am always striving to learn new things, and in my quest to attain ever more knowledge, I have found myself working in many different fields over the years: chef, education, client management, voice acting, translating, logistics, content creation etc. I've seen problems from many different angles, and through the lenses of many different professions and schools of thought.
Throughout all this I've noticed a trend. At some point, no matter in which field you work, there are always insights to be gleaned from relevant data. And I find myself drawn to the data and what secrets it may hold.
I started a business with my wife making freeze dried produce, fruits, vegetables, snacks, meals. My main contributions and responsibilities have been administrative processes, accounting, paperwork, systems development, data collection and analysis, mechanical maintenance, and website building. In the process, I've found myself implementing many past-acquired skills and learning a plethora of new one: Excel and SQL for data analysis, Python and Arduino for programming the freeze driers, physics, thermodynamics, circuitry, business relations, and the list keeps growing.
